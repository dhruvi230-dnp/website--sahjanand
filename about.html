Who We Are

Sahjanand Engicon LLP is a leading Electrical, Engineering, MEP, Infrastructure and Fabrication Contractor delivering innovative, reliable and high-quality solutions across industrial, commercial, government and infrastructure sectors.


Founded with a vision to provide reliable and cost-effective engineering solutions, the company began with electrical installation projects including street lighting, road-side electrification, residential wiring and cable laying works.


Today we provide complete engineering solutions including planning, design, execution, commissioning and long-term maintenance support.
